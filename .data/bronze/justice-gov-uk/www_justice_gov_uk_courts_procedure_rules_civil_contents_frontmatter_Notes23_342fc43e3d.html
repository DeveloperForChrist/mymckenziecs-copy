Are you OK with cookies?

We use small files called ‘cookies’ on www.justice.gov.uk. Some are essential to make the site work, some help us to understand how we can improve your experience, and some are set by third parties. You can choose to turn off the non-essential cookies. Which cookies are you happy for us to use?

Coming into Force

Rules

The above rule changes come into force on 31 May 2001, with the following exceptions:

Amendments to CCR Order 48B and CCR Order 48D on 1 June 2001

Parts 55, 56 & 57 on 15 October 2001

Practice directions

The above practice direction changes come into force on 31 May 2001 with the following exceptions:

Amendment to PD 48B on 1 June 2001

New PDs for Parts 55,56 & 57 on 15 October 2001

Forms

All Forms relating to new Part 55 come into force on 15 October 2001. It is not proposed to produce additional forms in respect of Part 56.
