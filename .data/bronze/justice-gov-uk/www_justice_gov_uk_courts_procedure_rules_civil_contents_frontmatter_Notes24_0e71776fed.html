NOTES TO ACCOMPANY SEPTEMBER 2001 24th MONTHLY SUPPLEMENT

Items to be deleted before or on October 15 2001

If you are likely to need to refer to any of the orders to be deleted before or on October 15 2001 you can still access the rules as they were at the 23rd Update by downloading them as a file in zipped format [link to /cpr_web/update.htm].

Items which come into force on October 15 2001

Please note that the amendments enclosed with this update (save for the very few exceptions listed below) do not come into force until October 15 2001. The changes involve not only the insertion of new text, but also remove a number of RSC and CCR Orders either completely or in part.

Items which come into force before October 15 2001

Amendments to rules 16 and 17 of RSC Order 77 and rule 15 of CCR Order 49 came into force on March 26 2001, but were omitted in error from the previous update.

Amendments to RSC Order 112 and CCR Order 47 replacing references to ‘blood tests’ with ‘scientific tests’ are amendments which came into force on April 1 2001 but were omitted in error from the 22nd update.

Coming into force

Rules

The above rule changes come into force on October 15 2001, with the following exceptions:

Amendments to rules 16 and 17 of RSC Order 77 and rule 15 of CCR Order 49 came into force on March 2001 and were omitted in error from the previous update.

Amendments to RSC Order 112 and CCR Order 47 replacing references to ‘blood tests’ with ‘scientific tests’ were omitted in error from the 22nd update and came into force on April 1 2001.

Practice directions

The above practice direction changes come into force on October 15 2001.

Forms

All forms relating to Part 55 and Part 57 come into force on October 15 2001. It is not proposed to produce additional forms in respect of Part 56.
