Are you OK with cookies?

We use small files called ‘cookies’ on www.justice.gov.uk. Some are essential to make the site work, some help us to understand how we can improve your experience, and some are set by third parties. You can choose to turn off the non-essential cookies. Which cookies are you happy for us to use?

Coming into Force

Rules

The following come into force on 26 March 2001:

  • Amendments to rules 6.24, 8.2A, 17.4, 19.5, 32.13, 43.2,
    44.16, 48.8, 48.9 and Part 10
  • New rules 19.5A, 19.8A and 40.20
  • The omission of rule 48.9 and RSC Order 15, rules 13A and
    16

The following come into force on the date of entry into force of s. 90 of the Access to Justice Act 1999 (anticipated to be 1 April 2001):

  • Amendments to RSC Orders 79, 109 and 116 and to CCR Order
    27

The following come into force on the date of entry into force of s. 23 of the Family Law Reform Act 1987 (anticipated to be 2 April 2001):

  • Amendments to RSC Order 112 and CCR Order 47

Practice directions

The following come into force on the date of entry into force of s.1 of the Race Relations (Amendment) Act 2000 (anticipated to be 2 April 2001):

  • Amendment to PD 2B

The following come into force on the date of entry into force of s. 6 of the Insolvency Act 2000 (anticipated to be 2 April 2001):

  • Amendments to Directors Disqualification Proceedings
    Practice Direction so far as they relate to disqualification
    undertakings

The following come into force on the date of entry into force of the Limited Liability Partnerships Regulations 2001 (anticipated to be 6 April 2001):

  • Amendments to Insolvency Proceedings and Applications under
    the Companies Act 1985 and Insurance Companies Act 1982 Practice
    Directions
  • Amendments to Directors Disqualification Proceedings
    Practice Direction so far as they relate to limited liability
    partnerships

Forms

Changes to Forms N210, N208C and EX80A come into force on 19 March 2001

All other amendments come in to force on 26 March 2001
